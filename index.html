This is a recipe for butter chicken:

Ingredients:

2 lbs. fresh chicken tenderloins, patted dry


Marinade:

6 oz. plain Greek yogurt

2 Tbsp. fresh ginger, grated

1 Tbsp. garlic paste (about 2 cloves)

1 Tbsp. vegetable oil

1 tsp. salt

1 lemon

1 tsp. chili powder

1/2 tsp. Garam Masala


6 Tbsp. butter, divided

1 small onion, diced

(Optional) 2 Tbsp. green chili (jalapeno or serrano), seeded and finely chopped

1 Tbsp. fresh ginger, grated

1 tsp. cumin

1 tsp. turmeric

2 tsp. sugar

1/2 tsp. Garam Masala

14 oz.(can) tomato sauce

1 cup heavy whipping cream


1. Remove the white tendons from the tenderloins. Cut the tenderloins into 1-inch chunks.

2. In a mixing bowl, combine all the ingredients for the marinade and whisk together. Add the chicken and stir to

coat. Set aside for 10 to 30 minutes to marinate

3. Heat the frypan over medium heat. Add 2 tablespoons butter and let it melt. Cook the chicken pieces 2 to 4

minutes on each side to get a nice golden color. Remove from pan and set aside. Work in batches if needed.

4. Add the remaining butter to the pan and let it melt. Saute the onion, 3 cloves of garlic, and green chili until soft

and fragrant. Add the ginger, cumin, turmeric, sugar, and 1/2 teaspoon Garam Masala. Cook, stirring often, to

warm the spices

5. Carefully pour in the tomato sauce and stir to combine

6. Add the chicken back into the pan. Reduce heat to a simmer and cover with a lid. Cook 5 minutes to cook through.

7. Remove the lid and pour in the heavy cream. Taste the sauce. Add salt if needed.

8. Turn off the heat. Plate with steamed rice and garnish with cashews, fresh cilantro, and a wedge of lime.

Easy Homemade Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup (40g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (65g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • Optional: 1/2 cup (90g) chocolate chips or chopped nuts for added texture

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square baking pan or line it with parchment paper.
  2. Melt Butter: In a medium-sized microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter in the microwave. Allow it to cool slightly.
  3. Combine Wet Ingredients: To the melted butter, add sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract. Mix well until fully combined.
  4. Incorporate Dry Ingredients: Sift in cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder into the wet mixture. Stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  5. Add Optional Ingredients: If using, fold in chocolate chips or chopped nuts into the batter.
  6. Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly. Place it in the preheated oven and bake for about 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs attached. Do not overbake, as brownies will continue to cook as they cool.
  7. Cool and Serve: Allow the brownies to cool in the pan for about 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your delicious homemade brownies!

These brownies are simple to make and incredibly tasty. You can also customize them by adding toppings like chocolate ganache, powdered sugar, or a scoop of ice cream. Enjoy your baking!
